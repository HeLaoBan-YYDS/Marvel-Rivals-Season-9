## What is the Cyborg Fighting Style?

The Cyborg fighting style is heavily inspired by the cybernetic enhancements of Franky from the One Piece series. It allows players to utilize laser beams, heavy metallic slams, and robotic thruster-assisted attacks. Unlike traditional martial arts in the game like Black Leg or Fishman Karate, which rely heavily on physical martial arts or elemental mastery, the Cyborg style blends high-tech machinery with raw martial prowess. This makes it an incredibly well-rounded choice for players who want to cover all their bases in battle.

In the current meta, players have access to five distinct fighting styles, each offering a V1 and an awakened V2 form. The Cyborg style is highly regarded for its balanced utility, offering both excellent close-quarters crowd control and long-range sniping capabilities.

## How to Unlock the Cyborg Fighting Style

To obtain the **haze seas cyborg** V1 style, you must first locate the training grounds where all the fighting style trainers reside. According to player experience, the trainers for Black Leg, Electro, Fishman Karate, Dragon Claw, and Cyborg are clustered in a central training zone, making it easy to browse your options.

Once you find the Cyborg Trainer, you can purchase the base V1 style for a flat fee of 50,000 Beli. This base version grants you three fundamental moves that are useful for early-game leveling but lack the raw damage and utility required for late-game content.

To truly unleash the power of this style, you must seek out the Sage of Combat to initiate the V2 evolution. The **haze seas cyborg** V2 evolution stands out because of its unique material requirement: Saturn's Ring, along with a hefty sum of 500,000 Beli. In comparison, other styles like Electro V2 require Raging Cores and Gems, while Dragonic V2 requires Dragon Hearts.

## Cyborg V1 vs. Cyborg V2 Moveset Breakdown

The transition from V1 to V2 is not just a simple stat boost; it completely overwrites your moveset with superior visual effects, larger hitboxes, and holdable mechanics. Below is a detailed breakdown of both movesets.

### Cyborg V1 Moveset

The V1 moveset is straightforward and focuses on single-target damage and basic gap-closing. It is ideal for players who are still leveling up their mastery stats.

| Move Name | Input Key | Description | Visual / Combat Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cyborg Smash** | Z Key | A heavy ground slam that damages nearby enemies. | Small shockwave effect |
| **Cyborg Bullet** | X Key | Fires a rapid mechanical projectile from the fist. | Single-target projectile |
| **Cyborg Tackle** | C Key | A forward dash that rams into the opponent. | Gap closer with knockback |

### Cyborg V2 Moveset

Upon evolving your style at the Sage of Combat, your moves are replaced with the "Radical" variants. These moves feature a new blue electricity aesthetic and offer much higher utility.

| Move Name | Input Key | Description | Special Mechanics |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Radical Smash** | Z Key | An upgraded ground slam with lingering electricity. | Stuns enemies in place |
| **Radical Beam** | X Key | A massive, continuous energy laser. | Holdable for sustained damage |
| **Radical Barrage** | C Key | A rapid-fire flurry of cybernetic punches. | Holdable for lock-in combos |

According to community reports, the ability to hold down the keys for Radical Beam and Radical Barrage drastically increases your total damage output. Holding Radical Beam allows you to channel a continuous laser beam, reminiscent of a classic anime energy blast, which is perfect for melting boss health bars from a safe distance.

## PvP and PvE Combo Strategies

Many players running a **haze seas cyborg** build recommend focusing your stat points heavily on Melee and Defense. Since the style relies on raw physical power and energy outputs, having a high melee stat is crucial for scaling your damage.

In PvP, a common combo string involves initiating with a gap closer or a weapon stun, followed immediately by Radical Smash to lock the opponent in place with its electrical residue. While they are stunned, activate Radical Barrage to melt their health bar, and finish the sequence by unleashing a fully charged Radical Beam as they try to retreat.

*   **PvP Stun Combo**: Weapon Stun -> Radical Smash (Z) -> Radical Barrage (C) (Hold) -> Radical Beam (X) (Hold to finish).
*   **PvE Mob Farming**: Group enemies together -> Radical Smash (Z) -> Step back -> Radical Beam (X) to pierce all targets.
*   **Boss Melting**: Wait for boss attack animation -> Dodge -> Radical Barrage (C) -> Radical Beam (X) for maximum stationary DPS.

In PvE scenarios, such as farming sea bosses or clearing waves of enemies, the massive AoE of Radical Smash combined with the piercing range of Radical Beam makes clearing mobs incredibly efficient.

## How Cyborg Compares to Other V2 Fighting Styles

When comparing the **haze seas cyborg** to other martial arts like Dragonic or Electro V2, it offers a highly balanced middle ground. While Dragonic (Dragon Claw V2) is often favored by PvP enthusiasts for its combo-heavy vortex pulls and high-damage kicks, the Cyborg style holds its own due to its superior ranged capabilities.

Electro V2 provides excellent group farming with its yellow lightning AoE, but lacks the single-target melt potential of Cyborg's Radical Beam. Fishman V2 offers great utility with its hydro-launches and trident pulls, but requires farming Sea Beasts for evolution materials. Ultimately, maximizing your **haze seas cyborg** damage output comes down to landing your stuns and managing your cooldowns, making it one of the most rewarding styles to master.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I get the haze seas cyborg fighting style?
To get the base version, you must find the Cyborg Trainer located in the fighting style training area and purchase it for 50,000 Beli. To upgrade it to V2, you must bring Saturn's Ring and 500,000 Beli to the Sage of Combat.

### Where do I find Saturn's Ring for the Cyborg V2 upgrade?
According to player experience, Saturn's Ring is a rare drop obtained by defeating specific high-level bosses or completing challenging raids. Make sure you are adequately geared before attempting to farm this item.

### Is the haze seas cyborg good for PvP?
Yes, the style is highly viable in PvP. The Radical Smash provides a reliable electric stun that allows you to transition into a held Radical Barrage, locking your opponent in place for massive damage.

### Can you hold down the moves for Cyborg V2?
Yes, both Radical Beam and Radical Barrage can be held down. Holding the keys extends the duration of the attacks, allowing you to deal continuous damage to your targets.
