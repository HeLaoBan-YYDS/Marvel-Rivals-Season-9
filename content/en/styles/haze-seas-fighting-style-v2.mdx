## Black Leg V2: The Azure Inferno

Black Leg remains a favorite **haze seas fighting style v2** option for players who prefer high-mobility, kick-based combat. Known for its iconic blue flames and rapid-fire strikes, the V2 version (often referred to as the "Leg of Fury") transforms standard kicks into explosive maneuvers. 

To obtain Black Leg V2, players must first own the V1 style (purchasable for roughly 50,000 to 500,000 Beli depending on the sea) and reach 300 Mastery. You will then need to hunt for Sea Beast Hearts or Phantom Lanterns. Community reports suggest that while Sea Beasts in the Second and Third Seas have a 25% drop rate for hearts, the Phantom Lanterns from the Ghost Ship are an alternative requirement in certain game versions.

### Black Leg V2 Move Showcase
*   **Demonic Crash:** A high-impact ground slam that deals massive AoE damage.
*   **Tempest Whirlwind:** A spinning kick that creates a vacuum of blue fire, trapping enemies.
*   **Stampede:** A flurry of kicks that finishes with a powerful knockback.
*   **Azure Inferno:** The ultimate move, coating the user in intense blue flames for maximum damage output.

## Electro V2: The Way of the Thunder Lord

For those who prioritize speed and stun potential, Electro offers a high-speed **haze seas fighting style v2** experience. Originally taught by the Bunny Girl in Udon Prison, the V2 evolution requires you to challenge the gods—specifically Enel (Raijin). 

To evolve Electro, you need 300 Mastery and two Raijin Cores. The good news for players is that the Raijin Core is typically a 100% drop from the Enel boss, meaning you only need to defeat him twice to satisfy the material requirement. However, instead of Beli, this style requires a payment of 500 Gems, making it a "premium" style that requires active participation in events or daily tasks.

## Cyborg V2: Engineering Destruction

Cyborg is a powerhouse **haze seas fighting style v2** for tanking and heavy artillery. It is one of the more expensive styles to acquire, with the V1 version costing 800,000 Beli. The V2 upgrade, known as the "Steel Titan," leans heavily into the mechanical theme, utilizing lasers and heavy ordinance to clear waves of enemies.

The Sage of Combat demands two Saturn Rings for this evolution. These are obtained by defeating the Saturn Boss. Similar to the Raijin Cores, these are a guaranteed 100% drop, though the boss fight itself is significantly more difficult than earlier encounters. You will also need 5 million Beli to finalize the transaction.

### Cyborg V2 Skillset
1.  **Radical Smash:** An overhead strike with a mechanical fist that shatters the ground.
2.  **Radical Beam:** A high-intensity laser fired from the palms, piercing through multiple targets.
3.  **Radical Barrage:** A rapid-fire sequence of explosions and punches that melts boss health bars.

## Dragon Claw V2: Draconic Mastery

Dragon Claw is arguably the flashiest **haze seas fighting style v2** currently available in the game. It combines the raw power of draconic energy with fluid, claw-like strikes. This style is unique because even the base version (V1) requires Gems (150 Gems) rather than Beli, marking it as an elite combat form from the start.

To reach the "Draconic Master" rank, you must collect two Dragon Hearts. These are dropped by the Dragon Hybrid boss with a 100% success rate upon defeat. Like Electro, the final evolution cost is 500 Gems. Players often choose this style for its "Draconic Vortex" move, which provides some of the best visual effects and hitboxes in [Roblox](https://www.roblox.com/games/13636253457/Haze-Piece-UPD-1) combat games.

## Fishman V2: The Tidal Striker

Fishman remains a staple **haze seas fighting style v2** for sea-based combat and versatility. While the showcase for this style is rarer in community circles compared to Dragon Claw, its utility in water-based environments is unmatched. 

The requirements for Fishman V2 mirror those of Black Leg: 300 Mastery and two Sea Beast Hearts. Because Sea Beast Hearts are a 25% drop, this style can actually be harder to "grind" than the styles with 100% boss drops. You will need to patrol the Second and Third Seas, taking down Sea Beasts until the RNG favors you. The monetary cost is the standard 5 million Beli.

## Pro Tips for Efficient V2 Farming

1.  **Server Hopping for Bosses:** Since bosses like Enel, Saturn, and the Dragon Hybrid have set spawn timers, server hopping is the fastest way to get your two required materials. Don't wait around on a single server if the boss is on cooldown.
2.  **Mastery Grinding:** The best way to hit 300 Mastery is to equip the style and farm high-health NPCs in the Second Sea. Use your style for the "finishing blow" to ensure maximum Mastery gain.
3.  **Gem Management:** Since Dragon Claw and Electro require 500 Gems each, save your Gems specifically for these evolutions. Avoid spending them on random fruit spins if your goal is to max out your fighting styles.
4.  **Sea Beast Hunting:** For Fishman and Black Leg, join a crew or a public server dedicated to Sea Beast hunting. Having multiple players makes the 25% drop rate less frustrating as you can clear beasts much faster.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I get haze seas fighting style v2?
To get a V2 fighting style, you must reach 300 Mastery on the V1 version, collect two specific boss drop materials, and then speak to the Sage of Combat in the Second Sea. You will also need to pay a fee of either 5 million Beli or 500 Gems depending on the style.

### Where is the Sage of Combat located?
The Sage of Combat is typically found in the Second Sea. Players often report finding him in the elevated areas near the Sea Restaurant or the main hub islands where fighting style trainers congregate.

### Which V2 fighting style is the best for grinding?
While "best" is subjective, many players prefer **Cyborg V2** for its massive AoE beams or **Dragon Claw V2** for its high damage and wide hitboxes. Both are excellent for clearing groups of NPCs quickly.

### Can I switch between V2 styles once I've unlocked them?
Yes. Once you have paid the initial evolution cost and met the requirements, you can switch back to that V2 style for free by talking to the respective trainer or the Sage of Combat. You do not need to farm the boss materials again.
