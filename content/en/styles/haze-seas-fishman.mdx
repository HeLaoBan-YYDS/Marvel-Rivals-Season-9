## How to Unlock the Fishman Fighting Style

Before you can unleash the power of the ocean, you must first locate the correct trainer. In the current version of the game, often referred to by the community as Sea Beast or Haze Seas, the trainers for various fighting styles are grouped in a central hub area. This makes it convenient for players to compare styles like Black Leg, Electro, and Cyborg before committing their hard-earned currency.

To begin your journey with the **haze seas fishman** style, you must find the Fishman Trainer. Unlike some of the more elusive secret styles, the Fishman trainer is relatively accessible if you have the funds. The initial cost for the Version 1 (V1) style is 50,000 Beli (or the equivalent in-game currency).

| Requirement | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Trainer Location** | Main Style Hub / Trainer Island |
| **Initial Cost** | 50,000 Beli |
| **Prerequisites** | None (V1) |
| **Best For** | Early to Mid-game grinding and crowd control |

Once purchased, you will gain access to the base moveset. While the V1 version is considered "regular" by many veteran players, it provides a solid foundation for those who haven't yet reached the requirements for the more advanced awakenings.

## The Path to Awakening: Unlocking Fishman V2

The transition from V1 to V2 is where the **haze seas fishman** truly begins to shine. This process is often called "Awakening" or "Ascension" by the player base. To upgrade your style, you must interact with the Sage of Combat NPC, who acts as the gatekeeper for all fighting style evolutions in the game.

The evolution is not free, and it requires specific rare materials that can only be obtained through high-level boss fights or world events. Specifically, players report needing "Sea Beast Hearts" (sometimes referred to as CB Hearts) and a significant amount of Gems or Beli.

| Requirement | Amount Needed | Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sea Beast Hearts** | 2 | Dropped by Sea Beasts / Bosses |
| **Currency (Gems/Beli)** | 500,000 | Questing and Farming |
| **Mastery** | Level 300+ (Recommended) | Using the V1 style in combat |

Once you provide these materials to the Sage of Combat, your "Evolution is complete," and you will unlock the V2 moveset. This version changes the visual effects of your attacks to a more vibrant, hydro-themed aesthetic and significantly increases your damage scaling.

## Fishman Karate V2 (Awakened) Showcase

The V2 version of the **haze seas fishman** style is a massive upgrade in terms of both visuals and mechanics. The moves become much more fluid, allowing for "blitz" style attacks that can catch opponents off guard in PvP scenarios. Community showcases highlight that these moves often involve tridents and high-speed water jets.

One of the standout features of the V2 style is the "Hydro Launch," which allows the player to dash through enemies, carrying them along for the ride. This is excellent for repositioning or setting up a combo with a secondary weapon or fruit ability.

### V2 Move Breakdown

*   **Hydro Launch:** This is the primary mobility tool for the V2 style. The player blitzes straight through the target, dealing damage and dragging the enemy with them. It is a highly effective "gap closer."
*   **Trident Strike:** A series of attacks utilizing a water-formed trident. This move has a wider reach than standard punches and deals significant piercing damage.
*   **Idle Descent:** A visually impressive finisher where the player leaps into the air and crashes down with a massive water explosion. Player experience suggests this is one of the "cleanest" looking moves in the game.

## Exploring Fishman Island: Quests and Bosses

To truly embody the **haze seas fishman** style, players should spend time on Fishman Island. This location is a vibrant, underwater paradise that serves as a high-level training ground. It is recommended for players between levels 1600 and 1700.

The island is ruled by the Neptune King, a formidable boss who uses accurate trident techniques. Defeating him is not only a rite of passage but also a way to obtain the legendary trident weapon, which pairs perfectly with the Fishman fighting style.

| Quest Giver | Level Req. | Objective | Reward |

Farming on Fishman Island is the most efficient way to gain the mastery needed to fully unlock the potential of your fighting style. The Fishman Elites are particularly good for training because they have high health pools, allowing you to cycle through your full rotation of moves multiple times.

## Tips for Mastering the Fishman Style

To get the most out of your **haze seas fishman** experience, you should consider your overall character build. Fighting styles in Haze Seas scale primarily with your Melee stat. If you are using this style as your main source of damage, ensure you are dumping points into Melee and Defense.

1.  **Use Haki:** Always ensure your Armament Haki is active when using Fishman Karate. This significantly boosts the raw damage of every punch and water strike.
2.  **Combo Starters:** Use Hydro Launch to initiate combat. Since it blitzes through the enemy, it often leaves them stunned for a split second, allowing you to follow up with Shark Barrage or a Trident Strike.
3.  **Hold Moves:** Some moves in the V2 moveset can be "charged" or held down to increase their duration or damage. Experiment with the timing to find the maximum output.
4.  **Race Synergy:** Pairing the Fishman fighting style with the Fishman race (Fishborne) provides additional passive buffs, such as increased swim speed and better water-based damage resistance.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I get the haze seas fishman style?
To get the **haze seas fishman** style, you must visit the Fighting Style Trainer island and locate the Fishman Trainer. You can purchase the V1 version for 50,000 Beli. To upgrade to V2, you will need to talk to the Sage of Combat and provide 2 Sea Beast Hearts and 500,000 currency.

### Is Fishman Karate better than Black Leg?
The choice between the **haze seas fishman** style and Black Leg depends on your playstyle. Fishman Karate offers better mobility and AoE (Area of Effect) attacks, making it great for farming. Black Leg often focuses on high-damage burns and single-target strikes. Most players find Fishman V2 to be more versatile for general gameplay.

### Where is Fishman Island located?
Fishman Island is a high-level zone typically recommended for players level 1600 and above. It is an underwater location that can be reached by traveling to the designated area on the world map. It is home to the Neptune boss and the quest giver Coral Grimes.

### What are the best stats for a Fishman build?
For a build centered on the **haze seas fishman** style, you should prioritize the Melee stat to increase your attack damage. Additionally, investing in Defense is crucial for survivability during boss fights like Neptune, and having a decent amount of Stamina will allow you to chain your V2 moves more effectively.
