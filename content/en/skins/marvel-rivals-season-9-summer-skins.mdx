## Major Gameplay Changes: Black Widow Rework & Team-Ups

Cosmetics are only half the story in Season 9. The developers are also implementing major gameplay changes designed to shake up the competitive landscape. Chief among these is the highly anticipated Black Widow rework. While some veteran Black Widow players have expressed frustration over changes to their favorite hero's kit, the general consensus is that the rework was necessary to address balance issues and improve her viability in high-level play.

| Feature | Old Team-Up System | New Season 9 Team-Up System |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ability Choices** | Single pre-determined team-up ability | Two distinct team-up abilities to choose from |
| **Anchor Requirement** | Required to have the anchor hero on the team | No anchor hero required to activate the basic ability |
| **Anchor Synergy** | N/A | Having the anchor hero on the team grants a stronger version |
| **Hero Flexibility** | Highly restricted team compositions | Increased freedom in choosing team compositions |

Additionally, the Team-up system is receiving a complete overhaul. Previously, players were restricted to specific team compositions to activate powerful synergy abilities. Under the new system, every hero has access to two different Team-up abilities that they can select during a match. Crucially, you no longer need a specific "anchor" character on your team to use these abilities. However, if the anchor character is present in the match, the Team-up ability receives a significant power boost. This change dramatically increases team composition flexibility while still rewarding thematic pairings.
