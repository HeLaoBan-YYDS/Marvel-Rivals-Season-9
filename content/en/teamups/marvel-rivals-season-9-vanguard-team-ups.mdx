## Hulk & Doctor Strange: The Gamma Powerhouses

Hulk remains one of the most versatile Vanguards in the game, and his Season 9 synergies make him incredibly difficult to bring down [1]. When paired with Wolverine, Hulk enters a "Furious State" where his fists glow red, boosting both his attack speed and movement speed [1]. According to player experience, the most clutch aspect of this duo is the crowd-control (CC) immunity. Once Hulk's health drops below his green regenerating bar into his main health pool, he becomes completely unstoppable, preventing enemies from pulling or stunning him [1].

Meanwhile, Doctor Strange players get an incredible boost when Hulk is on the field [1]. The *Gamma Maelstrom* team-up prevents Doctor Strange's stored energy from decaying over time, allowing him to hold onto his maximum damage potential indefinitely [1].

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the best marvel rivals season 9 vanguard team ups for beginners?
For beginners, the **Groot and Mantis** (*Vibrant Vitality*) or the **Hulk and Wolverine** (*Savage Slam*) team-ups are highly recommended [1, 2]. They offer simple, passive benefits like enhanced healing and crowd-control immunity that require very little mechanical coordination to get value from [1].

### Do I lose the base team-up effects if my partner dies?
No. In Season 9, many team-ups still grant a "base effect" even if your partner isn't nearby or is waiting to respawn [1]. However, having your partner alive and on the field unlocks the "enhanced effect," which drastically boosts the ability's power [1].

### How does Thor's cheat-death mechanic work with Hela?
When Thor takes fatal damage, he enters a temporary state of death defiance with a small slither of bonus health [1]. If Thor or Hela gets a kill during this brief window, Thor is fully revived with a generous chunk of health [1]. If they fail to get a kill, Thor dies as normal [1].

### Can Rogue use her Gambit team-up infinitely?
Yes! If Gambit is on your team, Rogue's kinetic explosion passive remains locked in and active on every single attack [1]. If Gambit is not on the team, the ability is balanced around a standard cooldown timer [1].
