## Devil Fruits Breakdown: Elemental, Natural, and Beast Types

Devil Fruits are the cornerstone of combat in this game. They grant players incredible powers, ranging from elemental control to physical transformations. When studying the haze seas wiki database, fruits are generally divided into three main categories: Natural (Paramecia), Elemental (Logia), and Beast (Zoan). 

Elemental fruits are highly sought after by beginners because they grant passive immunity to physical attacks from lower-level enemies. This makes grinding quests significantly easier, as standard mobs will not be able to damage you. 

Natural fruits offer unique utility and crowd control, while Beast fruits provide massive stat boosts and high durability. Based on player experience, here is how some of the most popular fruits stack up in the current meta:

| Fruit Name | Category | Primary Playstyle | Notable Abilities / Strengths |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Ice | Elemental | Crowd Control & PvP | Freeze enemies, walk on water, high AoE damage |
| Sand | Elemental | Grinding & Mobility | Sandstorms, high damage over time, fast travel |
| String | Natural | Fast-Paced PvP | High mobility, sharp slashing attacks, combo setups |
| Barrier | Natural | Defensive & Support | Create protective walls, reflect damage, block bosses |

If you are lucky enough to find the Ice or Sand fruit early on, prioritize leveling up your Fruit stat. Community reports suggest that Ice’s ability to freeze water completely changes how you navigate the map, saving you precious Beli on boats. 

String and Barrier fruits require more tactical play. String is excellent for PvP encounters due to its high speed and combo potential, while Barrier is ideal for group boss raids where keeping the boss distracted is key.
