## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I upgrade to the haze seas golden staff v2 in the new update?
To upgrade to the V2 version, you must possess the base Golden Staff (a 1% drop from the Thunder God on Sky Island), reach the required sword mastery level, collect rare upgrade materials like Lightning Essences, and present them to the weapon smith NPC along with Beli.

### Can I use the Golden Staff V2 while transformed into the God of Lightning?
No. When you activate the Electricity Fruit's "God of Lightning" transformation, your character grows in size and switches to a specialized fruit-only combat mode. You cannot actively swing the staff, but you still benefit from its passive damage buffs to your elemental skills.

### Does the Golden Staff V2 buff all devil fruits?
No, the specific damage buff is exclusive to the Electricity Fruit (also known as the Lightning Fruit). While you can use the staff with other fruits for its physical sword moves, you will lose out on the bonus synergy damage.

### What is the drop rate of the base Golden Staff?
The base Golden Staff has a 1% drop rate from the Thunder God boss located on Sky Island. Because of this low percentage, players recommend using drop rate boosts or farming in groups to speed up the process.
