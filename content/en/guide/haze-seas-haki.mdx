If you want to survive the dangerous waters of the Grand Line in this popular Roblox anime RPG, mastering **haze seas haki** is absolutely essential. These powerful abilities grant defensive dodges, damage multipliers, and the unique capability to strike elemental fruit users who would otherwise be immune to physical attacks. In this comprehensive guide, we will break down how to obtain and level up every form of **haze seas haki** so you can dominate both PvP combat and high-level PvE raids.

Whether you are a new player trying to land your first hits on elemental bosses or an advanced player preparing for the challenging endgame, understanding how these mechanics work will drastically improve your combat efficiency. Let's dive into the trainer locations, costs, and level requirements you need to meet.

## Unlocking Buso Haki (Armament)

When planning your progression, obtaining the armament form of **haze seas haki** should be your top priority. Known in-game as Buso, this ability coats your weapons and physical attacks in a dark, protective aura. It is the primary tool used to bypass the passive immunities of elemental (Logia) fruit users, making it indispensable for boss farming and PvP.

To learn this ability, you must locate the Buso Trainer NPC. The trainer can be found in two distinct locations depending on the level of the skill you are trying to acquire. 

### Buso Haki Level 1 and Level 2 Progression

The first level of Buso Haki can be purchased once you reach level 350. You will need to travel to Logue City to find the trainer. For the second level, which increases your damage output even further, you must travel to Revolutionary Island once you reach level 1150. 

| Haki Level | Trainer Location | Price (Beli) | Level Requirement | Damage Bonus (Sword & Combat) | Key Bind |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Level 1** | Logue City | $100,000 | Level 350 | +5% Damage & Elemental Bypass | J |
| **Level 2** | Revolutionary Island | $250,000 | Level 1150 | +7.5% Damage & Elemental Bypass | J |

According to player experience, saving up the $100,000 Beli by level 350 is relatively easy if you focus on completing local quests in Logue City. Make sure to toggle the ability on using the default hotkey "J" before engaging in combat, or the damage bonus will not apply.

## Observation Haki (Kenbunshoku) Locations and Costs

To dodge incoming damage automatically, you must unlock the sensory variant of **haze seas haki**. Observation Haki allows you to read enemy movements, highlighting opponents with a clear outline even through solid structures. More importantly, it grants you a set number of automatic dodges, allowing you to completely avoid damage from heavy attacks.

The Observation Haki Trainer is a wandering NPC who can be found at Marine Base, Bubble Island, or Desert City. Finding him requires a bit of travel, but the defensive utility he offers is well worth the journey.

### Observation Haki Level Upgrades

Upgrading your Observation Haki increases the number of active dodges you can perform before the ability goes on a cooldown. It also increases the clarity of your visual tracking.

| Haki Level | Trainer Locations | Price (Beli) | Level Requirement | Max Dodges | Key Bind |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Level 1** | Marine Base / Bubble Island / Desert City | $150,000 | Level 600 | 3 Dodges | R |
| **Level 2** | Marine Base / Bubble Island / Desert City | $200,000 | Level 1500 | 5 Dodges | R |

Community reports suggest keeping Observation Haki active during boss fights to avoid devastating one-hit-kill moves. Since the dodges recharge over time, managing your dodge meter is a crucial skill for high-level raids.

## Conqueror Haki (Haoshoku): The Ultimate Power

The rarest and most exclusive power-up in the game is the conqueror's variant of **haze seas haki**. Unlike Buso and Observation, Conqueror Haki cannot be bought from a standard NPC trainer using Beli. Instead, it represents a player's innate disposition to dominate.

When activated, Conqueror Haki unleashes a massive spiritual shockwave that stuns and knocks out weaker enemies in your immediate vicinity. However, players should note that this ability does not work on boss-level NPCs, meaning its primary use is for crowd control against mobs and asserting dominance in PvP matches.

### Methods to Obtain Conqueror Haki

Because this ability is so powerful, the methods to obtain it are highly restricted. The table below outlines the three known paths to securing this rare power.

| Obtainment Method | Requirements / Cost | Success Rate | Special Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Game Pass** | 3,499 Robux | 100% | Instant unlock upon purchase |
| **Defeating Admins** | Find and defeat an in-game Admin | Extremely Low | Highly dependent on server events |
| **D Clan Race** | Roll the "D" Clan race during character creation | Low Chance | Requires Race Spins |

Once unlocked, you can activate this ability using the "H" key. The visual effect will create a shockwave around your character, instantly incapacitating nearby minor enemies.

## Essential Movement Abilities: Flash Step and Sky Walk

While not technically classified under the traditional Haki umbrella, Flash Step and Sky Walk are vital movement abilities that directly complement your Haki build. Having high mobility allows you to position yourself perfectly to land Buso-enhanced strikes or escape danger when your Observation Haki dodges are on cooldown.

### Flash Step Upgrades

Flash Step allows you to teleport instantly to your cursor's location within a set radius. The trainer for this ability can be found on Clown Island (Level 1), Three Islands (Level 2), and Bubble Island (Level 3).

## Endgame Synergy: Pairing Haki with Gear 5

To maximize your **haze seas haki** builds, players often pair these buffs with top-tier fruits and combat styles. The absolute peak of combat power in the current meta is the Gear 5 (Sun God Nika) awakening. However, unlocking Gear 5 is one of the most grueling grinds in the game.

To begin this process, you must meet several steep requirements:
*   Reach Level 3000 or higher.
*   Have the Gum-Gum Fruit (Gum) equipped.
*   Have both Gear 2 and Gear 4 unlocked.

Once you meet these prerequisites, you must obtain a Dragon Orb, which has a mere 1% drop rate from the MaceV2 Boss. Using this orb, you can spawn the Dragon Superboss at Dragon Island (Onigashima). Defeating this superboss while having the Gum fruit equipped guarantees a 100% drop rate for the Drums of Liberation. 

After securing the drums, you must travel to a floating island near Dragon Island to speak with the Incurr NPC. This NPC will allow you to awaken your skills one by one.

### Gear 5 Skill Awakening Costs

| Awakening Step | Required Level | Resource Cost | Item Needed | NPC Location |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Per Skill Slot** | Level 3000 | $500,000 Beli & 100 Gems | Drums of Liberation | Floating Island (Dragon Island) |

Combining the raw destructive power of Gear 5 with the defensive capabilities of Observation Haki and the damage modifiers of Buso Haki makes your character virtually unstoppable in high-level raids. To test these strategies, you can jump into the game directly via the [official Roblox platform](https://www.roblox.com) and start grinding.

## Tips for Farming Beli and Gems Fast

Because upgrading all your Haki abilities and movement skills requires hundreds of thousands of Beli, you will need an efficient farming strategy. 

1.  **Prioritize Boss Quests:** Instead of grinding minor mobs, set your spawn point near bosses like the Clown Boss or the Mace Boss. Their quests yield significantly higher Beli and experience.
2.  **Server Hopping:** Once you defeat a boss, swap servers to find one where the boss has already respawned. This bypasses the natural spawn timers.
3.  **Daily Rewards and Codes:** Always check for active promo codes released by the developers, which frequently award free Beli, Gems, and Stat Resets.
4.  **Faction Buffs:** Joining a crew or faction can sometimes provide passive boosts to your money-making endeavors, making the grind to level 1500 much smoother.

By combining these farming methods, you can easily gather the funds required to purchase Buso Level 2 and Observation Level 2 as soon as you hit their respective level requirements.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I get haze seas haki in the game?
To get the basic forms of **haze seas haki**, you must visit specific trainer NPCs located throughout the map. For Buso (Armament) Haki, visit the trainer in Logue City once you reach level 350. For Observation Haki, locate the trainer at Marine Base, Bubble Island, or Desert City once you reach level 600.

### Can I upgrade my haze seas haki to higher levels?
Yes, both Buso and Observation Haki can be upgraded to Level 2. You must meet the higher level requirements (level 1150 for Buso Level 2 and level 1500 for Observation Level 2) and pay the required Beli to the respective trainers on Revolutionary Island or the Observation Haki locations.

### Does haze seas haki work on all enemies?
Buso Haki allows you to bypass the passive immunities of elemental fruit users, meaning it works on all enemies. However, Conqueror Haki only works on regular mob enemies and will not affect boss-level NPCs, though it remains highly effective in PvP encounters.
