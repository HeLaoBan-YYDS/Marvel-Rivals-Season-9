## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I start my first haze seas race evolution?
To begin the evolution process, you must first reach the minimum level required by the evolution NPC (often found in the Second or Third Sea areas). Once you meet the level requirement, locate the NPC corresponding to your race, complete their designated quest (which usually involves defeating specific mobs or collecting items), and pay the required amount of in-game currency.

### What is the rarest race in the game?
The rarest races fall under the Mythical category, which has an incredibly low roll probability of just 0.1%. Getting one of these races requires significant luck or a large number of race spins.

### Can I keep my evolved status if I roll a new race?
No. If you choose to roll a new race using your spins, your current race and its associated evolution progress will be overwritten. It is highly recommended to only evolve a race that you plan on keeping for a long time.

### Is the haze seas race evolution process permanent?
The evolution is permanent for that specific race on your character. However, as mentioned above, if you use a spin to change your race, you will lose that evolution. If you ever roll back to your previous race, you will have to start the evolution process from the beginning.
