## Major Gameplay Overhauls and Balance Changes

Season 9 is not just about cosmetic items and new maps; it also introduces some of the most drastic balance adjustments the game has seen. The developers are using this patch to address long-standing community feedback regarding character balance and the overall flow of combat.

### 1. The Team-Up Abilities Rework
The developers are rolling out a massive overhaul to the Team-Up system. Previously, some Team-Up abilities felt mandatory, forcing players into specific team compositions just to compete. In Season 9, Team-Up passive bonuses are being streamlined to offer more flexible playstyles, allowing players to mix and match their favorite heroes without feeling penalized for ignoring strict character pairings.

### 2. Black Widow Rework
Community reports have highlighted that Black Widow has struggled to find her footing in the high-tier competitive meta. To address this, Season 9 introduces a comprehensive rework of her kit. Her grapple mechanics will be much more fluid, allowing for better vertical mobility, and her ultimate ability is being redesigned to provide more utility for her team during coordinated pushes.
