## New Hero: Jubilee (The Dazzling Strategist)

Jubilation Lee, better known as **Jubilee**, is the headlining hero of the Season 9 launch. Classified as a **4-star difficulty Strategist** with **275 HP**, she brings a unique blend of pyrotechnic plasma and crowd-controlling utility to the support roster. 

In the lore of Season 9, Jubilee’s son, Shogo, has been trapped in a dragon form due to the ongoing Timestream Entanglement. Out of desperation to save him, she struck a deal with Apocalypse in Thebes, temporarily becoming his Horseman of Famine. Her gameplay kit beautifully merges her classic firework abilities with dark, vampiric powers inspired by her history in Marvel Comics.

### Jubilee's Complete Ability Kit

Jubilee's playstyle revolves around generating "Sparkle Marks" and utilizing attack speed boosts to rapidly heal her team while blinding aggressive flankers.

| Ability Name | Type | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Energy Plasmoids** | Primary Fire | Rapidly fires lumikinetic explosive light blasts (8 shots/sec, 48-round magazine) that heal allies and damage enemies. |
| **Sparkle Mark** | Passive | Applied by specific abilities. Detonating these marks with primary fire grants allies bonus health/healing and deals burst damage to foes. |
| **Blooming Balls** | Active | Deploys up to two energy spheres that heal allies and damage enemies in range. Shooting the balls increases their radius and duration. |
| **Dazzling Detonation** | Active | Detonates a Blooming Ball to blind and damage enemies, while healing and granting a speed boost to allies. Applies Sparkle Marks to all affected. |
| **Vampiric Energy** | Active | Wraps Jubilee in dark energy, boosting movement speed, jump height, and attack speed while making primary fire free to cast. |
| **Firework Finale** | Ultimate | Unleashes a massive ring of firework clusters that launch enemies upward, forming an orbiting field that heals allies and damages foes. |

### Jubilee's Team-Up Abilities

Jubilee launches with an incredibly strong Team-Up synergy featuring **Blade**. By deploying her **Vampiric Field**, allies standing within the designated zone gain life steal on their attacks. If Blade is actively on her team, this field is upgraded to provide a powerful, continuous healing-over-time effect to all friendly heroes standing inside.

## Mid-Season Hero: The Hood (The Clone-Wielding Vanguard)

Arriving later in the mid-season update (**Season 9.5**, scheduled around **August 10, 2026**), **The Hood** (Parker Robbins) will join the roster as the game’s 53rd playable character. 

Unlike traditional, shield-reliant tanks, community reports and datamined details suggest that The Hood will serve as an offensive, highly disruptive **Vanguard**. His kit is heavily centered around dark magic, dual-pistol gunplay, and confusing clone projections.

### Predicted & Datamined Kit for The Hood

According to early leaks and player experience reports from testing phases, The Hood’s kit focuses on high-risk, high-reward frontline chaos:

*   **Dual-Gun Shooting:** Rapidly fires mystical dual pistols, which are subject to an overheat mechanic.
*   **Cooling Shield:** A unique defensive barrier that ties directly into his weapon temperature and shield health management.
*   **Clone Projection & Detonation:** Projects mystical clones of himself forward. He can control their range and detonate them to deal massive area-of-effect damage, making it incredibly difficult for opponents to identify the real tank.
*   **Demon Transformation:** A powerful transformation state (likely his Ultimate) that channels the dark energy of his cloak to drastically increase his survivability and damage output.

### Seasonal Lore and Team-Up

In the Season 9 storyline, The Hood stands as a street-level crime boss who surprisingly goes head-to-head with Apocalypse himself. In Season 9.5, he will also receive a special vampire-themed Team-Up with Jubilee, transforming both characters into dark, monstrous forms on the battlefield.

## New Map: Thebes (Egyptian Convoy)

Alongside the introduction of these powerful **marvel rivals season 9 heroes**, a brand-new Egyptian-themed Convoy map called **Thebes** is scheduled to go live on **July 23, 2026**. 

This map is heavily tied to Apocalypse's domain. The layout features narrow alleys, grand temple structures, and vertical high-ground spots that are perfect for mobile heroes. 

### Key Strategic Areas in Thebes

To help your team coordinate during Convoy matches, keep these three key zones of the map in mind:

1.  **The Outer Ruins:** A wide-open starting area with minimal cover, favoring long-range Duelists and defensive Vanguards who can hold sightlines.
2.  **The Chamber of Judges:** A tight, indoor chokepoint where Strategists like Jubilee can easily bounce Blooming Balls and trigger massive blinds to halt the convoy's progress.
3.  **The Throne of Apocalypse:** The final payload destination, featuring high-ground platforms that allow vertical characters to rain down damage on the defending team.
