## Prepare for the New Map: Thebes

In addition to the **marvel rivals season 9 upcoming characters**, players will have a brand-new battleground to master. On **July 23, 2026**, a new Convoy map called **Thebes** will go live [1]. This Egyptian-themed map is heavily centered around the season's overarching villain, Apocalypse [1]. 

Thebes features tight choke points, vertical high-ground perches perfect for snipers, and open plazas where team fights will inevitably explode. Strategists like Jubilee will find plenty of cover to safely heal their allies, while highly mobile Duelists like The Hood can utilize the flank routes to ambush unsuspecting backlines.

### Tips for Dominating the Thebes Map

1. **Control the High Ground:** The ruins of Thebes offer massive vertical advantages. Ensure your team has mobile heroes who can contest these spots.
2. **Watch the Choke Points:** The convoy route passes through narrow archways. Save your high-impact ultimates, like Jubilee's Grand Finale, for these tight spaces [1].
3. **Utilize the Flank Routes:** Avoid running down the main path. Use the side tombs and alleyways to surprise the enemy team from behind.
