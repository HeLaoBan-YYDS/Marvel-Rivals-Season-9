## Decoding the All-Black Necrosword Lore

To understand why the community is so excited about **marvel rivals season 9 gorr the god butcher**, one must look at the history of his weapon. The All-Black Necrosword was the first symbiote, forged by Knull, the God of Symbiotes, using the celestial fire of a decapitated Celestial's head. In Marvel Rivals, this weapon has already been referenced in Gallery Cards as far back as Season 3.

The lore snippets from these cards describe the sword's sentience and its desire to be "revered" and "venerated." It explicitly mentions that while Knull was its creator, another wielder put the blade on a "pedestal of godhood." This is a direct reference to Gorr's transformation from a grieving father on a nameless planet into a cosmic executioner. The presence of the sword in the Season 9 trailer suggests that Gorr is not just a story-only character but a looming force that will likely join the roster to hunt the "gods" currently playable in the game, such as Thor, Hela, and Magneto.
