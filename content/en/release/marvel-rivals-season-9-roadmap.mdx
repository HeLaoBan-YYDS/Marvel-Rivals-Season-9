## New Hero Spotlight: Jubilee (Strategist)

Joining the roster on July 10 is the fan-favorite X-Man, Jubilee. Classified as a **Strategist**, Jubilee brings a unique mix of crowd control, area denial, and team utility to the battlefield. Instead of traditional healing, she utilizes her signature pyrotechnic energy plasmoids to shield allies and disorient enemies.

### Core Abilities:
*   **Fireworks Display (Primary Fire):** Fires rapid-fire energy sparks that deal light damage and apply a minor blind effect on direct hits.
*   **Prismatic Shield (Active Ability):** Throws a plasma bubble that absorbs incoming damage and converts it into healing over time for allies standing inside.
*   **Flashbang Sparkler (Utility):** Detonates a concentrated burst of light, interrupting enemy channel abilities and knocking back close-range threats.
*   **Supernova (Ultimate):** Jubilee unleashes a massive firework display that damages all enemies in a wide radius while boosting the movement and reload speeds of her teammates.

## The Team-Up Ability Overhaul

One of the biggest systemic changes detailed in the **marvel rivals season 9 roadmap** is the complete overhaul of the Team-Up system. Previously, Team-Up abilities were locked to specific character duos, which heavily restricted team compositions in competitive play.

Starting in Season 9, NetEase is introducing **Dynamic Team-Up Nodes**. Players can now select from a pool of generic synergy bonuses at the start of a match, allowing any composition to benefit from cooperative play. Classic character-specific Team-Ups (like Rocket Raccoon riding on Groot's shoulders) remain in the game but have been rebalanced to ensure they do not dominate the meta.

## New PvE Mode: Path to Doomsday

For players looking for a break from intense PvP matches, the **Path to Doomsday** mode makes its debut on July 24. This co-op PvE mode tasks teams of four with defending key generators against waves of Chrono-bots sent by Doctor Doom. 

Path to Doomsday features rogue-like elements, allowing players to upgrade their hero's abilities between waves. Completing the mode on higher difficulties rewards players with exclusive cosmetic titles, sprays, and points toward their Season 9 Battle Pass.
