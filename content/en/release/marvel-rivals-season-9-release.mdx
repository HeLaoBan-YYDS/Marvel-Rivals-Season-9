## Exploring the Mystery of Thebes Map

The new map, Thebes, is a sprawling Egyptian complex that blends ancient architecture with high-tech Celestial machinery. This map is divided into three distinct zones, each offering different tactical advantages. Players who have analyzed the **marvel rivals season 9 release** promotional materials have noted that the map features significant verticality, favoring heroes with flight or climbing abilities.

### Key Points of Interest (POIs)

1.  **The Chamber of the Judges:** A central hub where the "Seven Lights" mechanic is most prominent. This area is wide open, making it a playground for Snipers and Flyers.
2.  **The Mutant Spires:** Narrow corridors and high-ground platforms that offer excellent flanking routes for Duelists.
3.  **The Celestial Forge:** The final objective point, filled with environmental hazards like shifting sand and energy beams.
