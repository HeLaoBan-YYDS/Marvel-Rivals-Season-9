## Marvel Rivals Season 9 Battle Pass Overview

The Season 9 Battle Pass is officially titled **The Faith-Harvesting Engine**. It runs from July 10, 2026, until September 4, 2026, giving players just under two months to grind through the tiers. The pass leans into a dark, mythic narrative following the fall of Apocalypse, leading heroes to the ancient Egyptian-themed sands of Thebes.

Like previous seasons, players can choose between two premium purchase options:

*   **Luxury Battle Pass (990 Lattice):** Unlocks the premium reward track, giving you access to all exclusive skins, animated nameplates, and MVP animations.
*   **Upgraded Luxury Battle Pass (2100 Lattice):** Instantly boosts your progression by bypassing the initial tiers so you can jump straight into the action with high-tier rewards.

## Every Marvel Rivals Season 9 Battle Pass Skin

The Faith-Harvesting Engine battle pass features ten distinct character skins spread across 11 primary reward pages. This includes two highly coveted Legendary-tier skins that come complete with unique animations, custom visual effects, and animated nameplates. 

Here is the complete breakdown of every character skin featured in the [Marvel Rivals](https://www.marvelrivals.com/) Season 9 Battle Pass:

*   **Location:** Page 1 (Luxury Track)
*   **Theme:** Savage Land
*   **Associated Rewards:** "Tribal Tempo" Emote, "Fair Game" MVP Animation, "Primal Predator" Animated Nameplate & Spray

Elsa Bloodstone kicks off the battle pass at Tier 1 with a wild, prehistoric reimagining. ## Community Reception and Player Experience

Following early access showcases, the community response to the **marvel rivals season 9 battle pass skins** has been highly positive, though not without some constructive feedback. 

According to player experience reports on social media and Reddit, the visual designs of **Venom Bloom** and **Dead Mummy Deadpool** are considered absolute home runs. The creative detour of turning Venom into a carnivorous plant has been praised as one of the most innovative skins in the game's history.

However, some players have expressed minor disappointment regarding the duration of the MVP animations. A common piece of feedback among competitive players is that several MVP animations—such as Rocket Raccoon's "Second Dawn" and Iron Man's "Soaring Horus"—feel slightly too brief, often clocking in at under three to four seconds. Players are hoping that future patches might slightly extend these celebratory sequences to give top-performing players more time in the spotlight.

## FAQs About Marvel Rivals Season 9 Battle Pass Skins

### How do I unlock the marvel rivals season 9 battle pass skins?
To unlock the majority of the Season 9 battle pass skins, you must purchase either the Luxury Battle Pass (990 Lattice) or the Upgraded Luxury Battle Pass (2100 Lattice) and level up your pass by playing matches and completing seasonal challenges. However, Mister Fantastic's "Red-Y 4 Action" and Ultron's "Ultron-15" skins are available on the Free Track.

### When does Marvel Rivals Season 9 start and end?
Season 9, titled *The Faith-Harvesting Engine*, officially launches on July 10, 2026, and is scheduled to conclude on September 4, 2026. 

### Are the shop bundle skins exclusive to Season 9?
While shop bundles like the "Bloodless Berserker" Wolverine and "Raider Prime" Rogue are introduced at the start of Season 9, they are typically sold in the in-game store and may rotate back into availability in future seasons, unlike battle pass skins which are highly time-limited.

### What is the theme of the Season 9 Battle Pass?
The theme, "The Faith-Harvesting Engine," blends ancient Egyptian mythology (tying into the upcoming convoy map, Thebes) with primal, savage, and robotic elements.
