## Analysis of MVP Animations and Emotes

A point of contention within the community this season has been the duration of the MVP animations. In previous seasons, these cinematics were often 7-10 seconds long, showcasing the hero in a grand display of power. In Season 9, many of the animations, such as Iron Man’s "Soaring Horus" or Rocket’s "Second Dawn," clock in at under 5 seconds.

*   **Soaring Horus (Iron Man):** A quick flight toward the camera with Egyptian iconography.
*   **Bloom Appetite (Venom):** Venom devours a flower in a very brief, comedic sequence.
*   **Radiant Rebirth (Mantis):** A short sequence where Mantis channels solar energy.
*   **Mummy Moves (Deadpool):** A brief dance routine in his mummy wraps.

While the brevity allows players to get back into the matchmaking queue faster, some "whale" players and collectors feel that the "wow factor" has been slightly diminished. However, the quality of the "Biological Beats" emote for Ultron has been praised for its unique sound design and faithfulness to the character's robotic nature.
