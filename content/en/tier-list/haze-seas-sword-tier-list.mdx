## B-Tier & C-Tier Weapons: Mid to Early Game Gear

As you progress through the islands, you will naturally start with weaker gear. The B-Tier and C-Tier weapons are designed to help you level up your stats until you can farm for better equipment.

B-Tier weapons, such as the Krampus Scythe and the standard Fisherman Trident, offer decent baseline damage but lack the scaling needed for late-game bosses. C-Tier weapons represent the bottom of the barrel. The basic Katana and the standard Pipe are cheap and easy to acquire, but they should be replaced as soon as you reach higher-level islands. ## How to Maximize Your Sword Build

Simply equipping a top-tier sword is not enough to guarantee victory. To truly unlock the potential of the weapons listed on our **haze seas sword tier list**, you must optimize your character build.

First, focus on your stat distribution. If you are planning to run a sword-main build, you should heavily invest your points into the Sword stat. This directly increases the damage of all your weapon skills.

Second, pair your sword with a complementary Devil Fruit. For example, pairing a high-mobility sword like the Raiu Sword with a fruit that offers crowd control can make you an unstoppable force in PvP. Alternatively, using a fruit that groups enemies together makes AoE weapons like the Bisento V2 or SeaBeast Hammer V2 incredibly efficient for farming experience points.

Lastly, practice your combo timings. Many of the S-Tier weapons allow you to chain moves together. For example, using the White Blade v2's Midnight Flash to close the distance, followed by Trillion Slashes, can lock an opponent in place for a devastating finish.
