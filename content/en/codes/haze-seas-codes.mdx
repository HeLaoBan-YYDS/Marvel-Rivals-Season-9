## FAQ: Haze Seas Codes and Gameplay

### How often are new haze seas codes released?
New codes are typically released during major game updates, which occur every few weeks, or when the game reaches a new "Likes" milestone on Roblox. During holiday seasons like Christmas or Halloween, you can expect a higher frequency of limited-time rewards.

### Can I use haze seas codes more than once?
No, each individual code can only be redeemed once per Roblox account. Attempting to reuse a code will result in an "Already Redeemed" message. However, codes are account-wide, so the rewards will stay with your character even if you change your fruit or style.

### What is the best way to spend the cash from codes?
For beginners, the best use of cash is buying a basic sword or your first fruit spin. As you progress, you will want to save your money for upgrading your Haki abilities and purchasing better ships to navigate the more dangerous parts of the sea.

### Why did my code say "Invalid"?
An "Invalid" message usually means one of three things: the code has expired, there is a typo (check your capitalization!), or the server has not picked up the newest code list yet. Always double-check the spelling and try a fresh server before moving on.
