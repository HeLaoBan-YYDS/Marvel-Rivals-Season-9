## Mythical Tier: The Saturn (Spider) Fruit

The Saturn Fruit, often referred to by the community as the Spider Fruit, is a Mythical-tier powerhouse that focuses on crowd control and overwhelming AOE damage. Based on player experience, this fruit is currently considered one of the most "overpowered" options in the game due to its unique stun mechanics.

In its base form, the Saturn Fruit grants the user spider-like appendages and a custom M1 string. The most notable feature of the base M1 is the built-in stun; every hit momentarily freezes the target, making it nearly impossible for enemies to escape a full combo.

Furthermore, the "Saturn Swing" creates a localized black hole effect, pulling enemies into a central point for easy follow-up attacks.

## Mythical Tier: Okuchi-no-Makami (Yamato Fruit)

The Okuchi Fruit, inspired by the legendary wolf deity, is the premier choice for players who prefer a mix of elemental ice damage and high-speed physical strikes. Upon equipping this fruit, players gain a tail and a custom sprinting animation that reflects the beast's predatory nature.

This fruit is highly praised for its "Frozen Flames" and "Cold Breath" abilities. Unlike standard fire attacks, these ice-based moves apply a freeze debuff, allowing you to chain together long, devastating combos.

## Legendary Tier: The Wolf Fruit

Not every top-tier fruit needs to be Mythical. The Wolf Fruit is a Legendary-tier entry that has become a fan favorite for its "snappy" animations and high mobility. While it lacks the custom skywalk auras of the Mythical **haze seas zoan fruits**, its raw speed and low cooldowns make it a formidable choice for competitive play.

The Wolf Fruit excels in "hit-and-run" tactics. Its moves, such as the "Finger Minigun," allow for rapid-fire damage without the long wind-up times seen in heavier fruits.

## Strategic Tips for Zoan Users

1.  **Master the M1 String:** Unlike Logia users who rely on skills, Zoan users deal a massive chunk of their damage through basic attacks. Learn the rhythm of your custom M1s to keep enemies in a perpetual "hit-stun" loop.
2.  **Timing the Transformation:** Don't transform immediately at the start of a fight. Save your transformation (B key) for when you need to refresh your cooldowns or gain a sudden burst of health and defense to turn the tide.
3.  **Aiming Projectiles:** Moves like the Saturn Fruit's "Spider Web" or the Okuchi Fruit's "Cold Breath" can be aimed vertically. Use this to catch players who try to "skywalk" away from your ground attacks.
4.  **Mobility is Key:** Zoan transformations often change your dash and sprint animations. Practice the new movement speed to ensure you don't over-aim your attacks or dash into environmental hazards.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the best Zoan fruit in Haze Seas?
Currently, the Mythical Saturn (Spider) fruit is widely considered the best for both PvP and PvE due to its M1 stun mechanic and massive AOE "Spatial Annihilation" move. However, the Okuchi fruit is a close second for players who prefer ice-based crowd control and tracking projectiles.

### How do I get Mythical haze seas zoan fruits?
You can obtain these fruits by rolling at the Fruit Dealer in the Starter Island or other major hubs. They cost 5 Million Beli or 2,999 Robux. Because the spawn rate for Mythicals is extremely low, many players prefer to save their Beli and wait for the fruit to appear in the hourly stock.

### Do Zoan fruits work better in the First Sea or Second Sea?
While Zoans are powerful everywhere, they truly shine in the Second Sea. The higher health pools of bosses in the Second Sea require the sustained DPS that Zoan custom M1s provide. Additionally, the defense buffs from transformations are crucial for surviving the high-damage attacks of late-game enemies.

### Will the Wolf Fruit be upgraded to Mythical?
As of the current update, the Wolf Fruit remains a Legendary tier. While it is incredibly strong, there are no official reports from the developers suggesting a rarity tier change. Instead, the focus remains on adding new Mythicals like the Saturn and Okuchi fruits to the roster.
