This "risk vs. reward" mechanic is essential for keeping the gameplay loop engaging over long sessions. ## Community Expectations and Concerns

As with any highly anticipated release, the community is cautious. While the **haze seas trailer** focuses on the "wow" factor, veteran gamers are looking for substance. The most common concern is the "floatiness" of combat. If the swordplay feels disconnected or the ships feel like they are sliding on ice rather than cutting through water, the immersion will break.

Community reports indicate that players are looking for:
*   **Server Stability:** Maritime games often struggle with high-ping environments during large-scale naval battles.
